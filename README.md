# **NarrateAStory.com | Museum Technology | Digital Heritage | Eye-tracking for Audience Engagement Analytics**

A collaborative research platform exploring digital technologies for heritage documentation through ontologies and audience engagement analysis using eye-tracking.

## **Focus Areas**

- Developing crowdsourced datasets for the documentation of rare heritage
- Developing rare heritage ontologies for rare heritage using crowdsourced data
- Developing ontology mapping techniques and immersive storytelling experiences for rare heritage
- Developing low-cost eye-tracking systems for audience engagement analysis in heritage exhibitions

While developed for digital heritage research, these low-cost eye-tracking methodologies are designed to be extensible and adaptable to other behavioural analytics domains, such as social attention research in ADHD.

## **Reference**
Deb, B. (2024). *Collaborative Heritage Mapping through Publicly Sourced Triplets.* Primitive Tider, 26, 95–103. https://doi.org/10.5617/pt.12093
