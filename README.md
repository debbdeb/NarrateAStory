# **NarrateAStory.com | Museum Technology | Digital Heritage | Eye-tracking for Audience Engagement Analytics**

A collaborative research platform exploring digital technologies for heritage documentation using ontologies and audience engagement analysis through eye-tracking.

## **Focus Areas**

- Developing crowdsourced datasets for rare heritage documentation
- Designing ontologies for rare heritage using crowdsourced data
- Visualizing and mapping heritage ontologies
- Developing low-cost eye-tracking systems for audience engagement analysis in heritage exhibitions

While developed for digital heritage research, these eye-tracking methodologies are designed to be extensible and adaptable across domains, including social attention research in ADHD and drone navigation.

## **Reference**
Deb, B. (2024). *Collaborative Heritage Mapping through Publicly Sourced Triplets.* Primitive Tider, 26, 95–103. https://doi.org/10.5617/pt.12093
