# NarrateAStory.com

NarrateAStory.com is a collaborative research platform focused on data storytelling, museum technologies, and social attention.

In particular, it focuses on research in rare heritage curation using ontologies and user engagement analytics through eye-tracking.

Core research areas include:
- Crowdsourced co-creation of rare heritage ontologies
- Web mapping and visualization of co-created heritage ontologies
- Attention and engagement analytics in human interaction environments
- Low-cost eye-tracking systems for user engagement analysis

The platform develops rare heritage ontologies and investigates user engagement in heritage information.

It is designed as a domain-extensible framework and also applicable to other domains such as social attention research.

Reference:
Deb, B. (2024). *Collaborative Heritage Mapping through Publicly Sourced Triplets.* Primitive Tider, 26, 95–103. https://doi.org/10.5617/pt.12093
